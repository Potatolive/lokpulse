Let’s play … with politics … for a change!

LokPulse aims to make politics fun.

To make things clear,
LokPulse is not a political party.
LokPulse is not a government organization.
LokPulse is not a news media firm.
LokPulse don’t fancy herself to be Wiki-leaks.

We are just bunch of people of who are interested in games and technology. What started as fun fantasy league for Tamilnadu elections 2016 (by name 234seats.in) opened our eyes into the world of politics. We had so much fun in creating the game and saw enthusiastic participation from thousands of people across India. It was indeed an eye-opener for us to know that so many young folks are interested in Politics. And we thought why not keep this going and make it even more fun. LokPulse happened.
